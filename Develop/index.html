The Ocean

The ocean is a vast and mysterious place, covering more than 70% of the Earth's surface. It is home to a diverse array of marine life and holds many secrets waiting to be discovered.

Rubbish in the Ocean

Plastic pollution has become a major threat to marine life, with millions of tons of waste entering the oceans each year. This pollution harms wildlife, disrupts ecosystems, and can even enter the human food chain.
